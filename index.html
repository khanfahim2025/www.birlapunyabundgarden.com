Welcome to Birla Punya

Birla Punya, developed by Aditya Birla Real Estate Ltd, is a prestigious residential project in Bund Garden, Central Pune, offering meticulously designed 2, 3, and 4 BHK homes. Spanning 5.76 acres, the community features 80% open spaces and uninterrupted views of the river and city. A grand 5-acre clubhouse serves as the heart of the development, complemented by over 45 premium amenities, including a 2.5-acre managed lake. With Vastu-compliant units and a low-density design, this project is thoughtfully crafted by the renowned builder Broadway Malyan, ensuring luxury, serenity, and comfort for its residents.

About Aditya Birla Real Estate Ltd

Aditya Birla Real Estate Limited, established in 1897, has grown rapidly in various industries, including real estate and the pulp and paper industry. Century Pulp and Paper (CPP) is a leading manufacturer of high-quality writing and printing paper, tissue and board, and Rayon Grade Pulp products. The company has a prime-grade tissue paper plant and sheet-cutting facility in Lalkuan, Sonipat, Bharuch & Baddi. In 2016, Birla Estates entered the real estate sector to deliver exceptional residential and commercial spaces. The company's platinum-certified commercial properties, Birla Aurora and Birla Centurion, have become iconic in the commercial property fraternity.
